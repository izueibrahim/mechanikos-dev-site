## Goals

The main objective of **Bulan Malaysia Sihat Sejahtera** is to create awareness among Malaysians that leading a healthy and joyful lifestyle is not only easy but also crucial for improving their overall well-being.

## Insights

**1 in 2 Malaysians are overweight with 1 in 4 being physically inactive.** The primary issue is that Malaysians find it tedious and uninteresting to maintain a healthy lifestyle, leading to a lack of motivation.

## Approach &amp; Solutions

**Bulan Malaysia Sihat Sejahtera (BMSS)** is designed to foster a societal change to a healthier nation. In order to encourage Malaysians to adopt a healthier lifestyle, Bulan Malaysia Sihat Sejahtera had to make living healthy enjoyable and captivating. To ensure the success of this campaign, we had to be on all levels of the marketing funnel to reach as many people as possible.
